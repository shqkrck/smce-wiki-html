Catalogue - SMCE and SOCRATES case studies

This page tracks concrete cases where SMCE and/or SOCRATES have been applied in policy appraisal, impact assessment, or closely related public-decision settings.

It is different from Catalogue - downloaded SMCE and Better Regulation library. That page lists what is already stored locally. This page lists the main cases worth learning from, including official Commission impact-assessment examples and frequently cited field applications.

Why this page matters

Case studies are where the method becomes operational. They show: - how problem definition and objectives are linked to criteria - how option sets are made comparable - how stakeholder and equity perspectives are represented - how sensitivity and robustness work is used to prevent overconfident rankings

High

What the 2025 JRC review says these cases teach

From 20 years of SMCE in policy assessment - source note and the downloaded review: - SMCE aligns well with impact-assessment requirements when introduced early enough - the method is strongest when it helps structure data collection, option comparison, and stakeholder consultation - transparency is a recurring strength - common challenges include power dynamics, representation, accessibility, and resource demands
