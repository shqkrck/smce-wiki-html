Master bibliography

This page consolidates the three bibliography catalogues for the SMCE wiki into one structured reference page.

Use it as the main reading list for:

  • official EU and JRC sources on Better Regulation, impact assessment, scrutiny, and SMCE
  • methods and academic foundations for SMCE, MCDA, participation, equity, and robustness
  • AI governance and AI-assisted policy support sources relevant to public-sector appraisal

Recommended reading order

If you want an efficient path through the literature, read in this order:

  1. European Commission, Better Regulation Guidelines, SWD(2021) 305 — for the institutional workflow and expectations.
  2. European Commission, Better Regulation Toolbox — for practical analytical templates and evidence requirements.
  3. JRC, SOCRATES Manual (2022) — for the main official SMCE-oriented operational reference.
  4. JRC, 20 years of Social Multi-Criteria Evaluation in policy assessment (2025) — for policy applications and historical context.
  5. Munda (2004), Social multi-criteria evaluation: Methodological foundations and operational consequences — for the core logic of SMCE.
  6. Munda (2008), Social Multi-Criteria Evaluation for a Sustainable Economy — for the broader theoretical and practical framework.
  7. Dodgson et al. (2009), Multi-criteria analysis: a manual — for hands-on public-sector application.
  8. Reed (2008), Stakeholder participation for environmental management — for participation design.
  9. Sen (1979), Equality of What? — for equity framing.
  10. Azzini & Munda (2025), Sensitivity and Robustness Analyses in Social Multi-Criteria Evaluation of Public Policies — for robustness testing.
  11. Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (AI Act) and the Ethics guidelines for trustworthy AI — for AI governance constraints on AI-assisted policy support.
