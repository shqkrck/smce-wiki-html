Catalogue - official EU and JRC sources

This page catalogues official European Commission, EUR-Lex, and JRC sources relevant to SMCE, EC impact assessment, Better Regulation, Regulatory Scrutiny Board, stakeholder consultation, and the wider policy-evaluation process.

Use this page as the main entry point for authoritative references.

How to read this catalogue

  • Priority = how central the source is for understanding the EC policy cycle and where SMCE may fit.
  • Type = page, guidance, toolbox, communication, annual report, or JRC publication.
  • Relevance note = why the source matters for this wiki.
