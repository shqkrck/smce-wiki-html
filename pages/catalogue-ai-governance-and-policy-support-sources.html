Catalogue - AI governance and policy support sources

This catalogue gathers official and academic sources relevant to AI-assisted policy analysis, trustworthy AI governance for public-sector decision support, and methodological safeguards for using AI in impact assessment and evaluation workflows.

Related pages:

  • AI governance for impact assessment
  • AI-assisted policy analysis
  • Policy making process - impact assessment

How to use this catalogue

Use these sources for three closely related purposes:

  1. designing AI-supported analytical workflows for policy appraisal and evaluation
  2. setting governance requirements for public-sector decision support
  3. building safeguards around transparency, documentation, human oversight, auditability, and robustness

Working implications for this wiki

Across these sources, the most reusable safeguards for AI-assisted impact assessment are:

  • keep humans accountable for problem framing, option design, weighting, interpretation, and final recommendation
  • document intended use, non-intended use, data provenance, assumptions, and known limitations
  • preserve audit trails for prompts, model versions, datasets, parameter choices, and revisions
  • test robustness through sensitivity analysis, comparison against non-AI baselines, and adversarial checking
  • separate analytical assistance from automated decision-making
  • ensure transparency and contestability whenever AI affects evidence synthesis, scoring, or prioritisation
  • align AI use with existing Better Regulation and public-law obligations rather than treating AI governance as a standalone add-on

Notes

  • JRC publication pages were identifiable through search results, but the JRC repository applied anti-bot protections during collection. Handle URLs are included because they are the most stable official references.
  • OECD publication landing pages may enforce bot restrictions from this environment; where needed, the catalogue uses stable OECD or OECD-affiliated landing pages that expose the publication metadata.
